The Service

Find the product hiding in your audience.

90 minutes. $500. One written brief at the end. You walk away with the Tier 1 asset your community has already told you they want, and a plan to sell it.

Five quick questions, then the calendar.

What you get

One call, one brief, one asset.

01

A working call, not a sales call

90 live minutes where we identify the specific product your audience is already asking for. Your positioning, their signal, the gap between the two.

02

A written brief within 48 hours

The product, its positioning, the audience evidence that proves demand, and the first sell. You can hand it to a builder or build it yourself.

03

A pricing frame, not a vibe

Your first price, the floor you refuse to go below, and the tier structure that lets the product scale past one buyer.

How 90 minutes breaks down

The conversation has a shape.

  1. 00 – 20

    Your audience, in detail

    Where they live, what they ask for, what they have already bought from you or from adjacent creators. The ground truth of your community, not the Instagram bio version.

  2. 20 – 60

    The asset

    We name the exact product hiding in that signal and pressure test it against your positioning. If the first candidate fails, we iterate until we find one that survives the pressure.

  3. 60 – 90

    The first sale

    The offer, the price, the channel, and the sequence that gets it live in 14 days. You leave with a plan that does not need a marketing team to execute.

The fit

Who this is for, and who it is not.

This is for

  • Creators with 10K+ across platforms who are tired of renting attention
  • People with a clear audience pattern but no product of their own
  • Builders who can ship once they know what to build
  • Anyone who has an audience asking for something they are not currently selling

This is not for

  • Creators under 10K total. Come back when you have more signal.
  • People shopping for a marketing agency or a course, this is a product-definition call
  • Anyone looking for a done-for-you build, I hand you the brief, you own the build
  • Anyone who cannot name one thing their audience has asked for in the last 30 days
